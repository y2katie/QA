Fundamentals: What is Page Meaning?

Page Meaning Explained

Page meaning is how browsers, assistive technologies, and search engines understand the purpose and relationships of content on your page.

üè† Structural Meaning

"This is the main navigation"

"This is the primary content area"

"This is supplementary information"

üìù Content Meaning

"This is a heading that introduces a section"

"This is a list of related items"

"This is a quote from another source"

üîó Relationship Meaning

"This heading describes the following content"

"These form fields are grouped together"

"This button controls that content area"

Semantic Elements: Technical Deep Dive

HTML5 Semantic Elements by Category

<header>

Introductory content or navigational aids

<header role="banner">
  <h1>Site Title</h1>
  <nav>...</nav>
</header>

QA Test: Should contain site/section title or navigation

<main>

Primary content unique to the page

<main role="main">
  <!-- Unique page content -->
</main>

QA Test: Only one per page, contains primary content

<aside>

Content indirectly related to main content

<aside role="complementary">
  <h2>Related Articles</h2>
  <!-- Sidebar content -->
</aside>

QA Test: Can be removed without affecting main content

<article>

Self-contained, reusable content

<article>
  <header>
    <h2>Article Title</h2>
    <time datetime="2024-01-15">Jan 15</time>
  </header>
  <p>Article content...</p>
</article>

QA Test: Makes sense when extracted from page context

<section>

Thematic grouping with heading

<section aria-labelledby="products-heading">
  <h2 id="products-heading">Products</h2>
  <!-- Related content -->
</section>

QA Test: Should have a heading or aria-label

<time>

Machine-readable dates and times

<time datetime="2024-01-15T14:30:00">
  January 15, 2024 at 2:30 PM
</time>

QA Test: datetime attribute should be valid ISO format

<cite>

Reference to cited work

<blockquote>
  <p>"Innovation distinguishes..."</p>
  <cite>Steve Jobs</cite>
</blockquote>

QA Test: Should reference the source, not the quote itself

<details>

Disclosure widget with summary

<details>
  <summary>System Requirements</summary>
  <p>Windows 10 or later...</p>
</details>

QA Test: Should work without JavaScript

ARIA & Enhanced Meaning

ARIA Patterns for Page Meaning

aria-labelledby

Creates relationship between element and its label

<section aria-labelledby="products-title">
  <h2 id="products-title">Our Products</h2>
  <!-- Section content -->
</section>
Screen Reader: "Our Products, region"

aria-describedby

Links to additional descriptive content

<button aria-describedby="save-help">Save</button>
<div id="save-help">
  Saves your progress automatically
</div>
Screen Reader: "Save button, Saves your progress automatically"

role="group"

Groups related form controls or content

<div role="group" aria-labelledby="address-heading">
  <h3 id="address-heading">Billing Address</h3>
  <input type="text" placeholder="Street">
  <input type="text" placeholder="City">
</div>
Screen Reader: "Billing Address group"
